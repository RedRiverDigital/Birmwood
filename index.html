Birmwood is a dark medieval RPG in a Slavic inspired setting.
Play as the Drifter Parcival Godrigson; a charlatan, scavenger
and hired sword - chasing after a man known only by name.
Explore a living, open world and immerse yourself in the rich fiction, based on Slavic lore.
The terror and beauty of Birmwood is yours to unveil.
